# Performance/battery

- disable spotlight: 
  
    sudo launchctl unload -w /System/Library/LaunchDaemons/com.apple.metadata.mds.plist

- disable addressbook sync:

    launchctl unload -w /System/Library/LaunchAgents/com.apple.AddressBook.SourceSync.plist
